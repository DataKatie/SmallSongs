Poet Biographies

Editor and Contributor: Tamara DeDominicis

Tamara DeDominicis is a poet who listens to the whispers of the wind and the roaring of the tides. In the spring, she can usually be found beneath the boughs of blossoming cherry trees. Tamara's work has been published in many anthologies including Three Line Poetry, and 50 Haikus

Contributor: Katie Sullivan

Katie has been writing poetry since high school when she enrolled in the school's only Friday afternoon extra-curricular activity: creative writing club. She counts T.S. Elliot, Virgina Woolf, and Leonard Cohen among her many inspirations. While she still enjoys poetry, these days she prefers expressing her words in the form of songs, as an aspiring folk singer/songwriter.

Contributor: Jenni Suppa

Jenni has had a love of the English language, since she first discovered the joys of writing parody songs to the tune of "American Pie" by Don McLean. Although her poetry is often funny in nature, she has also discovered an enjoyment of words reflected in nature.

Contributor: Angelica Barrone

Angelica enjoys writing short poetry forms, as well as longer novels, specifically targeted to young adult readers. Angelica enjoys taking part in NaNoWriMo, the novel-writing month of November. When she can't be writing, Angelica keeps herself busy teaching elementary school students!

Do you want to be featured on our author page? It's easy! Just write a short poem and submit it, along with a short biography (no more than 1000 characters please!) You may also upload your photo. If your poetry is selected to be part of the magazine, we will add your information to the biography page. Are you ready to submit your original poetry? If so, please head to submissions.
