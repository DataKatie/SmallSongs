Poetry can also be expressed using music. Writing original songs requires construction of lyrics as well as a corresponding melody. Below are two original songs written by Katie Sullivan.

While traditional song-writing uses original music and lyrics, parody is a time-honored tradition where a pre-existing song is alerted with new lyrics to create a new and often amusing meaning.
